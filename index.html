This is my portfolio of work over the last Quarter for ICT-4560. Mr. O'Toole is the best!

Week 4 Assignment: Image Composition

Composited the photo of a person standing on a ledge to a photo with Lava in the background. Created two separate layers to be able to mesh these two images together. Helpful in learning how to cut and paste objects into other canvases.

Week 5 Assignment: Postcard

I used the photo that I had created the previous week and I decided to put a rectangular blue box on the bottom. From there, I decided to use the Text tool to select a slick looking font that would fit the tropical theme. I then decided to add in a vector-shape palm tree to add more spice.

Week 7 Assignment: Painting with the Mixer Brush

Utilized the mixer brush to be able to transform a painting of my Dad into a lifelike looking picture. I picked similar looking colors to draw paint strokes, zooming in and out to get a properly sized brush so I could properly draw over the picture.

Week 8 Assignment: Web Cinemagraph's

Took a picture of Cheesman park and created a layer mask to cut out the sky. Than I imported a stock video that I could use as the background. Finally, I overlayed the initial image with the sky video and then I used the timeline tool to equalize the length of the gif.

Week 9 Assignment: 3D Logo

Created a logo for a potential company using PhotoShop's 3D tool. I started off with the plain horizontal text tool. Afterwards, I downloaded a texture a created a layer over the initial text that included the texture. Then, I rendered my text in 3D mode, and utilized the X, Y, and Z rotation cues to properly position my logo. Finally I used the Threshold tool to make the texture look more realistic with my logo.
